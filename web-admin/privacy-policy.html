Thugil Designers — custom tailoring & design app
Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how [Your Legal / Business Name] ("we", "us", "Thugil Designers") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Thugil Designers mobile application (the "App") and related services. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account details: your mobile phone number (verified by SMS one-time password) and/or email address, and your name.
  • Delivery details: postal addresses (label, street, city, state, PIN code, optional landmark and approximate location) you add for order delivery.
  • Order information: garment categories, measurements/preferences, and notes you submit when placing tailoring orders.
  • Uploaded content: fabric photos and images you upload to generate or request designs.

Information collected automatically

  • Device & app-integrity data: to verify requests come from a genuine app on a genuine device and to prevent abuse, we use Google Play Integrity and standard device identifiers provided by Google services.
  • Usage & diagnostic data: basic logs and error reports needed to operate and secure the service.

2. How we use your information

  • Create and secure your account and sign you in (phone/email verification).
  • Accept, process, match to tailors, fulfil, and deliver your orders.
  • Generate AI-assisted designs from fabric images you upload.
  • Operate credits, plans, and subscriptions associated with your account.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Provide customer support and send service-related notifications.

3. Third-party services we use

We share the minimum data necessary with trusted providers who process it on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase Authentication — phone/email verification and account sign-in.
  • Amazon Web Services (S3) — secure storage of uploaded fabric images and generated designs.
  • Cloud hosting & database providers — to run our backend and store account/order data.
  • Payment provider — if and when in-app payments are enabled, payment details are handled by the payment provider; we do not store full card details.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, and meet legal or accounting requirements. When no longer needed, data is deleted or anonymised.

5. Your rights & account/data deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by emailing [support@yourdomain] from the email/phone associated with your account, or by using the in-app account option where available. We will delete your account, profile, addresses, uploaded images, and order history, except records we are legally required to retain.

You may also request access to or correction of your personal data using the same contact.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures (encrypted transport, access controls, and app-integrity verification) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

7. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will remove it.
